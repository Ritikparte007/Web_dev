Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

How long does it take to complete a website?

Simple websites take 3-5 days, while complex projects require 7-14 days. The timeline depends on your specific requirements, features needed, and project complexity. I always provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What are your payment terms?

I require 50% advance payment to start the project and the remaining 50% upon delivery. I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or other secure payment methods. All payment terms are clearly outlined in the project contract.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes! I provide 3 months of free support after project delivery. This includes minor changes, bug fixes, and technical assistance. Extended support packages are also available for ongoing maintenance needs.

Do you handle domain and hosting setup?

Absolutely! I can help you set up domain registration and hosting (additional costs apply), or I can work with your existing hosting provider. I'll guide you through the entire process to ensure everything is properly configured.

Can you work with my existing design or do you create new designs?

I can work both ways! If you have existing designs, I can implement them perfectly. If you need new designs, I create modern, user-friendly interfaces that align with your brand and business goals. Design consultation is included in all projects.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Django, Node.js, Python, JavaScript, and mobile app development. I also create games using Unity and web-based game engines. I always choose the best technology stack for your specific project needs.
